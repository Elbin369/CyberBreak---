HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

Mobile: Tap and drag anywhere horizontally on the screen to slide the paddle.

Desktop: Move mouse to guide the paddle, or use ← and → arrow keys / A and D.

Launch Ball: Ball launches automatically after 2 seconds.

BRICKS

Standard Bricks: Break in 1 hit.
Armored Bricks: Require multiple hits (cracks form!).
Explosive Bricks: Explode and damage adjacent blocks.
Solid Barriers: Cannot be broken normally (laser works!).

POWER-UPS

W Wide Paddle: Increases paddle size.
M Multi-Ball: Generates 2 additional balls.
S Sticky Paddle: Catches the ball on contact.
L Laser Gun: Shoot lasers to destroy bricks (Space/Tap).
F Fire Ball: Pierces through bricks without bouncing.
U Bottom Shield: Forcefield blocks balls from falling once.
